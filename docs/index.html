About Grace

Grace Hlavacek

Grace is a BFA 1 Experience Design and Production Major from Cheyenne, Wyo. Growing up, Grace was introduced to art through her mother, who has a large passion for photography. Grace cultivated that love for pictures and the memories they create and continues to love to take pictures at every moment. Currently, most of her work focuses on scenic design, along with some work in photography and web designs. Grace even coded most of this website herself! In addition to designing, Grace also enjoys and studies dance in her free time.
